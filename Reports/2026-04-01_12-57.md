## Root Causes / Key Findings

**Thread pool exhaustion (fixed in 1.8.0):** 8 article-processing threads × synchronous PDF downloads (up to 58 PDFs per article × 60s timeout) = hours of blockage. Fix: separate background ThreadPoolExecutor for PDFs.

**ArXiv PDF failure (fixed in 1.8.3):** `download_file` used a module-level `requests.Session` with only `User-Agent`. ArXiv serves an HTML landing page unless `Accept: application/pdf` is sent. Fix: per-request `Accept` header for document downloads only.
