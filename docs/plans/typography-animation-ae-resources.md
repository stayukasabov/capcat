Research date: 2026-03-08

## Tier 1 — Most Effective (Start Here)

### School of Motion — Kinetic Typography in After Effects (3-Part Series)
**Free | schoolofmotion.com**

The most referenced free series for kinetic typography in AE. Covers:
- Precomposing and composition structure
- Syncing text animation to audio with layer markers
- Camera moves and 3D space
- Professional workflow

- [Part 1](https://www.schoolofmotion.com/blog/kinetic-typography-after-effects-part-1)
- [Part 2](https://www.schoolofmotion.com/blog/kinetic-typography-after-effects-part-2)
- [Part 3](https://www.schoolofmotion.com/blog/kinetic-typography-after-effects-part-3)

Also available free via Class Central:
- [Part 1 on Class Central](https://www.classcentral.com/course/youtube-kinetic-typography-in-after-effects-part-1-134147)

---

### School of Motion — Text Animators Deep Dive
**Free | schoolofmotion.com**

Three focused tutorials on AE's native text animator system — the most powerful (and most ignored) built-in tool for typography animation.

- [Text Animators in After Effects](https://www.schoolofmotion.com/blog/after-effects-text-animators) — fundamentals, part of "30 Days of AE"
- [Creatively Using Text Animators](https://www.schoolofmotion.com/blog/after-effects-text-animator-tapered-stroke) — advanced creative use by Kyle Hamrick
- [Text Animation with Expression Controllers](https://www.schoolofmotion.com/blog/mastering-text-animation-expression-controllers-after-effects) — combining text animators with expressions for reusable rigs

## Tier 2 — Paid Courses Worth Considering

### Domestika — Typography Animation with After Effects
**Paid | ~$15–$30 on sale | Domestika**
Instructor: Érico Santana (clients: Heineken, Comic-Con Experience)

- 17 lessons, 3h 14m
- 5,434 students, 99% positive reviews
- Requires basic AE knowledge
- Focuses on animating phrases, giving text personality through motion

Links:
- [Course page](https://www.domestika.org/en/courses/1806-typography-animation-with-after-effects)
- [Reviews](https://www.domestika.org/en/courses/1806-typography-animation-with-after-effects/reviews)

**Paid | ~$15–$30 on sale | Domestika**

## Recommended Learning Path

1. **School of Motion — Text Animators** (free) — understand the native system first
2. **School of Motion — Kinetic Typography Part 1–3** (free) — apply it to a real project with audio sync
3. **Domestika — Mat Voyce** (paid, on sale) — if you want expressive, personality-driven typography motion
4. **School of Motion — After Effects Kickstart** (paid) — if you want a complete AE foundation alongside typography
