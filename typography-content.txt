On the web, good typography creates hierarchy, establishes tone, and guides users through content.\n\n### Core Typography Concepts\n\n#### Legibility\nHow easily individual characters can be distinguished\nCharacter recognition\n\n#### Readability\nHow easily text can be read and understood\nContent comprehension\n\n#### Hierarchy\nVisual order and importance of text elements\nContent structure\n\n#### Consistency\nUniform typography patterns throughout design\nDesign system\nKey Principle: Typography should be invisible-users should read your content without noticing the typography itself. Good typography serves the content, never overshadows it.\n\n## Type Scale\nA type scale is a progression of font sizes that creates visual hierarchy and consistency.
